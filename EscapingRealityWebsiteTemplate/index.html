Stranded(Alien EDITION)

An Alien about to hunt you !!!

You are a human sent out to explore the galaxy, but your ship has been damaged and you have crash landed on an alien planet. You must collect items to repair your ship and escape the planet. However, there are many dangers on this planet, so be careful! There are many things you should pay attention to in order to ensure your survival:

  1. Be wary, as you only have 5 hearts. At 0, you can expect to die.
  2. Collect Scraps to fix your ship.
  3. Once all scraps are collected, find your ship and repair it.
